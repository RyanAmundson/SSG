Help

     To search, in the left-hand column to the table below, click on specific sub-issues you are interested in - you can select more than one category, and your selections will be highlighted. Then, in the middle column, select what type of organization you are interested in. When you make your selection, the right-hand column will provide additional filters that you can use to further limit your search. Click on the filters you would like to include.


     If at any time you want to remove a selection, just click on the item in the table. Review your selections by evaluating the items listed just below the table. Finally, to complete your search, select Policy Actors to obtain the list of stakeholders, or alternatively, Laws and Agreements to obtain a list of governance mechanisms. You can toggle between Policy Actors and Laws and Agreements by clicking on the appropriate button and clicking the Update button.
